To cancel orders that aren't dispatched yet:

1.Go to Your Orders
2.Select the item you want to cancel and click
3.Cancel items
4.Provide reasons for cancellation (optional)
5.Click on Cancel Checked Items

Note:

Your order will be cancelled instantly and a refund will be processed to your original payment method, if the payment was already made. Learn more about refund processing timelines here. To cancel an order that has already been dispatched: Go to Your Orders Select the Request cancellation option and proceed further The item(s) will be returned to us for a refund (if the payment is already made) Note: In case you're still contacted for delivery, please refuse to accept it.
